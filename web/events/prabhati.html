Prabhati/প্ৰভাতী/प्रभाती - The Dawn of Vital Outlook

There is no denying the fact that unscientific and inaccurate information about menstruation is rife in many parts of India. Millions of girls and women still don't have adequate knowledge regarding the reproductive tract infections caused due to ignorance of personal hygiene during menstruation time. For a girl to be able to reach her full potential, it is crucial that she is educated on dealing with the biological issues that may sometimes emerge as a major complications like cervical cancer, urogenital infections and many more. In this regard, WakeWithWellness has decided to organise an awareness session, "Prabhati/প্ৰভাতী/प्रभाती - The Dawn of Vital Outlook" which targets girls in their early teens. The session shall cover the most critical aspects of the subject matter along with the distribution of free sanitary napkins , refreshments and stationeries.

Venue : Jalukbari Girls High-School, Guwahati
Date : 29th November, 2021.
